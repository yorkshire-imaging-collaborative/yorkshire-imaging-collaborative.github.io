**Typical Indications**

* Nodule or opacity follow up

**Contrast**

Non Contrast

**Timing + Field of View (FOV)**

Timing not applicable

FOV- Apices - Costo-phrenic angles

**Suggested MPR**

1mm axial soft tissue

1mm Lung

**Adaptations**

Can be limited to area of nodule if requested by Radiologist
